NEURONEST - Empowering Neurodivergent Minds with AI

---

## Overview

NeuroNest is a web platform designed to support neurodivergent children, including those with ADHD, autism, and dyslexia, along with their caregivers.

The platform focuses on improving communication, making learning more engaging, and helping parents better understand their child’s behavior through simple, accessible tools.

---

## Problem Statement

Neurodivergent children often struggle with:
- Understanding social cues  
- Expressing themselves clearly  
- Adapting to traditional learning systems  

At the same time, parents and caregivers face challenges in:
- Interpreting behavioral patterns  
- Finding reliable, real-time guidance  
- Using tools that are often scattered and not integrated  

---

## Our Approach

NeuroNest brings these needs together into a single platform with two interfaces — one for children and one for parents.

It combines guided interaction, personalized learning, and behavioral insights to create a more supportive and structured environment.


## Key Features

### Social Coach
- Presents real-life social situations (e.g., conflicts, conversations)  
- Allows children to input responses and choose a tone  
- Provides feedback and suggested improvements  
- Helps children practice communication in a low-pressure setting  

---

### Learning Engine
- Uses a child’s interests (such as dinosaurs or space) as input  
- Generates simple learning content and activities around that topic  
- Aims to make learning more engaging and relevant  

---

### Dyslexia Support
- Includes interactive exercises such as:
  - Word-to-image matching  
  - Phonetic recognition  
  - Syllable-based activities  
- Designed to support reading and language development  

---

### Parent Dashboard
- Displays basic activity data such as sessions and usage patterns  
- Highlights tendencies and learning preferences  
- Suggests simple strategies for better support  

---

### Connect Child
- Allows a parent to link with a child’s account using an OTP  
- Enables viewing of activity and insights  

---

## Product Structure

### Child Interface
- Simple and visual  
- Focused on interaction and learning  

### Parent Interface
- Focused on insights and guidance  
- Minimal and easy to navigate
